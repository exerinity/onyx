☆ Welcome again!

Hey! I'm exerinity ("ecksa-rinity"), an Australian ♂ self-taught programmer. I mostly make really weird web-based apps or things, but also sometimes Discord bots and other serverside stuff, so basically a full stack developer. I hate that fucking cheesy-ass title. Anyway, I code in JavaScript only. Unconventionally.


I play too many games—BeamNG.drive mostly. My favourite car in it is the Bruckell Bastion 5.7 Luxe AWD. I also sometimes play Counter-Strike 2 and GTA V.


I also listen to a ton of music. I love high-energy music, especially big room EDM from the early/mid 2010s. My favourite artists are Martin Garrix, Daft Punk, Avicii, Simon Viklund, deadmau5, Ummet Ozcan... just check here, I guess?


My main project is Voxity, a local music player written in vanilla JavaScript. It has... a lot of weird and/or cool features, and also, an Electron version that has some extra features. Learn more?

¿ So, what is this website?

This website, codenamed "Onyx", is an experimental retro-styled site; I have no idea why it exists, especially when exerinity.com has a ton of stuff, from a scripted starry background engine (its not images, its rendered in realtime), random songs, my Discord presence live... but who knows. I'm very experimental, I suppose. maybe this site might replace what I have now.
